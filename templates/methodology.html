The Engine

Our Methodology

How our highly orchestrated multi-agent framework produces tier-1 business intelligence.

1

Ingestion & Validation

Your raw product idea is ingested into our secure backend. The prompt is structurally analyzed by our orchestrator node to extract core technological requirements and presumed market sectors.

2

Parallel Agent Execution (CrewAI)

Four autonomous agents are spun up asynchronously. The Market Analyst pulls macro-economic data, while the Intel Analyst maps out the current competitive software landscape. Simultaneously, Customer Insights digs into forums.

3

Neural Synthesis

The Senior Business Analyst agent receives the raw vector outputs from the four research agents. It utilizes Google Gemini's massive context window to cross-reference data points, resolve contradictions, and format a unified investment memo.

4

Delivery

The final markdown response is sanitized, injected into our beautiful UI, and rendered securely into your browser session in less than 3 minutes.
